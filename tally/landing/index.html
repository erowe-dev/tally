How it works

Three steps to stop
leaving value on the table

01
Add your balances
Enter your current point totals for each program. Tally stores them locally — no account needed to get started.
Amex MR: 75,000 · Chase UR: 52,500 · Bilt: 18,000
02
Tell Tally where you're going
Enter your origin, destination, cabin class, and passenger count. Or choose hotel and pick your category. Tally detects the route region automatically.

03
Get a specific recommendation
Tally ranks every applicable transfer option by real cents-per-point value, marks which programs you can already cover with your balance, and tells you what to do next.
